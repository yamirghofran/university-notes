> The amount of light per surface area reaching the image sensor

[Pinhole cameras](/computer-vision/optics/the-pinhole-camera) capture very little light. They require long exposure time, so moving objects become blurry.

![Slide2-Image Formation, p.19](../attachments/slide2-image-formation-p19-0015f0c0.png)

This problem gets solved (though with a tradeoff) by using [Lenses](/computer-vision/optics/lenses).
