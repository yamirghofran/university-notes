---
title: Transpose Matrix
---

Transpose of a matrix is a new matrix rotating the rows of the given matrix.
Matrix $$A = \begin{bmatrix} 
1 & 2 & 4 \\ 
8 & 2 & 1 \\ 
7 & 3 & 0 \\ 
2 & 1 & 2 
\end{bmatrix}$$

then transpose of this matrix is 

$$A^T =\begin{bmatrix} 
1 & 8 & 7 & 2 \\ 
2 & 2 & 3 & 1 \\ 
4 & 1 & 0 & 2 
\end{bmatrix}$$

## Properties
$$(A^T)^T = A$$
$$(A + B)^T = A^T + B^T$$
$$(A - B)^T = A^T - B^T$$
$$(kA)^T = kA^T$$
$$(AB)^T = B^TA^T$$
$$(A^T)^{-1} = (A^{-1})^T$$
